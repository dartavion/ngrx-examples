A test for NGRX nested ofType in effects

Open the console and watch for logs

Outcomes

  • Click the second button first -- take notice that no log is emitted
  • Click the first button then the second -- all works fine

What is happening is the top level ofType must fire at least 1 time to have the nested ofType register and start listening for it's action.

Suggestion...use more actions and do not nest to avoid the inner ofType never catching it's action.
